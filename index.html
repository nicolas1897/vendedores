About this Project

The main goal of our project is to push and improve the bussiness of small florist in Colombia by giving them a tool to reach customers directly and avoid big companies as intermediaries.

So how do we Plan to Achieve this?

We have develop the first part of a mobile app that will allow the direct interaction between sellers and buyers and also the option to sell and purchase throug the app. (Once completed)
